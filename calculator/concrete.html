About Concrete Volume

Calculate the volume of concrete needed for various construction shapes. All measurements are in meters.

Rectangular Slab

Volume = Length × Width × Depth

Common for: Foundations, patios, driveways

Circular Slab

Volume = π × Radius² × Depth

Common for: Round patios, foundations

Cylindrical Column

Volume = π × Radius² × Height

Common for: Support columns, posts

Conversion Guide

1 cubic meter = 1,000 liters

1 cubic meter ≈ 35.3 cubic feet

1 bag (20kg) ≈ 0.011 cubic meters

1 cubic meter ≈ 90-91 bags (20kg)

Note: Add 5-10% extra for waste and spillage. Always order slightly more than calculated to account for uneven surfaces and over-excavation.
