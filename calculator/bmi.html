About BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It's calculated using the formula:

BMI = Weight (kg) / Height (m)²

BMI Classification

BMI Range Classification Health Risk
< 18.5 Underweight Possible nutritional deficiency or underlying health issue
18.5 - 24.9 Normal Weight Healthy weight range
25.0 - 29.9 Overweight Increased risk of health problems
30.0 - 34.9 Obese Class I Moderate obesity, higher health risk
35.0 - 39.9 Obese Class II Severe obesity, significant health risk
≥ 40.0 Obese Class III Morbid obesity, very high health risk

Note: BMI is a screening tool and doesn't directly measure body fat. It may not be accurate for athletes, children, elderly, or those with high muscle mass. Always consult with a healthcare professional for health advice.
