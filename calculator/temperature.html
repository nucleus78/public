About Temperature Conversion

Celsius and Fahrenheit are two common temperature scales. Celsius is used in most countries, while Fahrenheit is primarily used in the United States.

°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

Celsius to Fahrenheit

°C = (°F - 32) × 5/9

Fun Fact

-40°C and -40°F are the same temperature! This is the only point where both scales have the same value.
