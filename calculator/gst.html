About GST

Australia GST: Goods and Services Tax (GST) is a broad-based tax of 10% on most goods, services, and other items sold or consumed in Australia.

GST Rate: 10%

GST = Price × 0.10

Price Including GST = Price Excluding GST × 1.10

New Zealand GST: Goods and Services Tax (GST) is a value-added tax of 15% on most goods, services, and other items supplied in New Zealand.

GST Rate: 15%

GST = Price × 0.15

Price Including GST = Price Excluding GST × 1.15

Note: GST rates are correct as of 2024. Some goods and services may be exempt or zero-rated. Always consult with a tax professional for specific advice.
