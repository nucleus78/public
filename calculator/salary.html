About Salary Calculations

This calculator converts salary amounts between different payment frequencies, accounting for working days, holidays, and vacation time.

Unadjusted Values

  • • Based on 52 weeks per year
  • • 260 weekdays per year
  • • No vacation/holiday deductions
  • • Used for: Hourly, Daily rates

Adjusted Values

  • • Accounts for holidays
  • • Accounts for vacation days
  • • More realistic take-home
  • • Used for: Weekly, Monthly, etc.

Common Payment Frequencies

• Biweekly: Every 2 weeks (26 pay periods)

• Semi-monthly: Twice per month (24 pay periods)

• Monthly: Once per month (12 pay periods)

• Quarterly: Every 3 months (4 pay periods)

Note: Calculations assume standard working year. Actual pay may vary based on employer policies, paid time off, and specific holidays observed.
